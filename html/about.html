ABOUT

Malcolm Semedo Barreto is a student and interactive media designer based in Bulle. He began his art studies at the Académie de Meuron in 2022, where he was able to thrive in the field of 3D modeling. Passionate about 3D animation, he specialized in this area while exploring various aspects of digital creation. Curious and versatile, he experiments with multiple roles, both in animation and within the production workflow. During the foundation year at ECAL, he had the opportunity to explore different disciplines within Media and Interaction Design, including UI/UX design and creative coding.
